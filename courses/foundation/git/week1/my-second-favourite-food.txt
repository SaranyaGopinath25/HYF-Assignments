Garlic Cheese Toast

Ingredients

1 pod garlic
▢¼ cup butter, softened
▢½ tsp mixed herbs
▢½ tsp chilli flakes
▢½ tsp salt
▢2 tbsp coriander, chopped
▢5 slices bread
▢½ cup cheese, grated

Instructions
 
Firstly, roast 1 pod garlic on direct flame.
Roast on medium flame till the garlic is cooked well.
Cool completely, and mash the garlic really smooth.
Add ¼ cup butter, ½ tsp mixed herbs, ½ tsp chilli flakes and ½ tsp salt. Mix well.
Also add 2 tbsp coriander and mix well, making sure everything is well combined.
Spread the garlic butter on a bread slice and top with cheese.
Toast ina  pan on low to medium flame by covering.
Cook till the cheese melts.
Finally, cut it in half and enjoy the Garlic Cheese Toast Recipe.
