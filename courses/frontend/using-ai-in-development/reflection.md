*ETHICAL ISSUES, RISKS ASSOCIATED WITH USE OF AI*

*RISK*

1. **Over-reliance on AI**: Relying too heavily on AI for code reviews and improvements may lead to a lack of critical thinking and problem-solving skills in developers.
2.  **Security Risks**: AI-generated code suggestions may cause security vulnerabilities if not properly reviewed and tested by human developers.
3. **Incorrect code suggestions**: AI may sometimes provide incorrect code like outdated practices or deprecated APIS, which can lead to bugs if not carefully cross-checked with the latest documentations.


*HOW TO MITIGATE IN OUR OWN USE OF AI*
- Always review and test AI-generated suggestions and use them as a tool to enhance our work rather than a replacement for our own judgment and expertise.
- Continuously update our knowledge and skills to evaluate and implement AI suggestions.
